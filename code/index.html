Rubber Duck FAQ

What are rubber ducks?

A rubber duck (also known as a rubber ducky) is a toy shaped like a stylized duck, generally yellow with a flat base. It may be made of rubber or rubber-like material such as vinyl plastic.
– Wikipedia

Why rubber ducks?

Rubber ducks naturally inspire water play that develops muscle strength and coordination. With their bright color, smooth texture, and (for some) squeaky or quacky sounds, rubber ducks sharpen toddlers’ senses. Their presence in the bathtub soothes youngsters’ fears of water and water immersion and makes good clean fun of the routine hygiene they are learning.
– Wikipedia

When is a rubber duck no longer a rubber duck?

Here at Rubber Duck Inc. we believe that as long as it has the rough shape of a rubber duck, including a bill, it can be a rubber duck if it wants to.
